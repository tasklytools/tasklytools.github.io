Welcome to Taskly Tools — your go-to source for free, all-in-one online tools. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how your information is treated when you interact with our website.

Slogan: All-in-One Free Tools — Use It, Get Results, We Store Nothing.

1. Introduction

At Taskly Tools, your privacy is not just a promise—it’s part of how we operate. Our platform allows users to use a wide variety of tools without requiring account creation or personal information. We do not collect or store any files, data, or content that users submit to our tools for processing.

2. No Data Storage Policy

We want to make it clear:
✅ We do not save, log, or store any information entered into our tools.
✅ We do not keep any uploaded files—everything is processed instantly and deleted automatically after use.
✅ We do not track or link personal information to any tool usage.

Your data belongs entirely to you, and you are in full control when using our services.

3. Use of Cookies

While we do not use cookies to collect personal data, our advertising partners (like Google AdSense) may use cookies to display relevant ads based on your browsing behavior.

You can manage cookie settings through your browser or opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://www.google.com/settings/ads

4. Third-Party Advertising

Our website is monetized through Google AdSense. These third-party advertisers may use cookies, web beacons, or similar technologies to serve relevant ads. However:

  • We don’t have control over how these services gather data.
  • We also don’t have access to any personal information that advertisers may collect through their ads.
  • Any concerns about third-party data practices should be directed to their respective privacy policies.

Google’s ad policy can be reviewed at: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4.1 Affiliate Marketing Disclosure

In addition to Google AdSense, Taskly Tools participates in affiliate marketing programs, including the Amazon Associates Program. This means we may include product links in our articles that track purchases through cookies. If you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission—at no additional cost to you.

Affiliate partners like Amazon may use cookies and similar technologies to understand your interaction with their services. These cookies are governed by their own privacy policies.

We do not collect or store any personal data related to affiliate links. However, we encourage you to review Amazon’s privacy policy for further information:
https://www.amazon.com/gp/help/customer/display.html?nodeId=468496

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. These links are offered solely for your convenience and to provide additional information. Once you leave our site, we are no longer responsible for how your data is handled on external platforms.

We recommend checking the privacy policies of any third-party websites you visit via our links.

6. Children’s Privacy

Taskly Tools is intended for general audiences and does not intentionally collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has shared personal data with us, please get in touch, and we will take immediate action to remove it.
